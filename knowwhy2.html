What Makes Heart Disease So Deadly?

1. Global Prevalence

Heart disease is a global pandemic, affecting millions across continents. The World Health Organization reports that cardiovascular diseases claim approximately 31% of all global deaths, making it the ultimate health challenge of our time.

2. Silent Progression

Like a stealthy predator, heart conditions develop silently over years. By the time symptoms emerge, significant damage may have already occurred, turning early detection into a critical lifeline.
